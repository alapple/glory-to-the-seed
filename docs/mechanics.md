## Mechanics
While playing, the player is able to move freely across his fields and plant his crops.  1  
While planting crops or taking care of your soil, you may find rare valuables dug in the soil. These can then be exchnaged for Rubbles.  1
With Rubbles players can employ employees, they can buy perks like quicker harvest speed, harvest machines.  2
Seasons that influence the crops viability. Some crops grow better in the cold, some in hot climates.  4
When buying items for the farm, you always have to think ahead due to the travel time of the delivery truck. The truck always arrives once a day.  2

Random Events:
Every now and then, a robber come could by and steal some of your harvest, to prevent this you could build a fence.  1
Insects can infest the harvest which then results in a loss of harvest. To prevent this you can buy pesticides to protect your crop.  2
A virus wave hits your farm and infects your workers, this results in a loss of efficiency for a period of time. To prevent this, you can buy medicine  3
