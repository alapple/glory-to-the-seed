## Event Writing

## Fire
Mother Nature herself is personally attacking your farm with fire! You must act quickly to save as much of your crops as possible! Wait... maybe this has upsides?

## Hungry Neighbor
A hungry neighbor has decided to steal a major part of your harvest! You must report him to the red army to be able to reach the quota! Or do you...???

## Worker Mood Swings
Your workers are getting moody and lazy... you could feed them potatoes to regenerate their energy, or let them starve. Upto you...!

## Worker Strike
OH NO! The workers are executing a strike! You must negotiate with them to get them back to work, or else your production grinds to a halt!

## Pest Infestation
A mysterious western man has infested your crops with pests! You must act quickly to save your harvest! Do you want to contact your red friends?

## COVID-12 Outbreak
A new foreign strain has hit your farm and infected your workers! The majority are down with symptoms and cannot work! This will surely affect your production rate!
